Back in 1993 an agent, having heard me do an uninvited reading at a magazine launch, said that if I had a book she and I might be able to make some money out of it. So I jammed a bunch of things together into something about the right length and sent it to her. She was quite kind in her reply but did suggest that I maybe didn't actually know what a book was.
